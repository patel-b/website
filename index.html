Synthesis of Fibrous Silica Nanoparticles

In all the recent talks about climate change, CO2 molecules present in the atmosphere are a major contributor to global warming. One method of removing the carbon dioxide molecules from the atmosphere are by a technology called adsorption. I dwelved deeper into this topic by executing a project to synthesize amine functionalized fibrous silica nanoparticles to entrap atmospheric CO2. Currently, silica nanoparticles are being synthesized by one standard silica source (TMS-EDTA) but my goal was to see if starting with a different silica-base containing amine functional groups would affect the structure and morphology of the results.
